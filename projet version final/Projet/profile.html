About me

Currently, I am pursuing my B.S. in Biology at the University of North GA. My ongoing passion for animals, led me to multiple years in animal care. In addition, having worked in several kennels, provided me with exposure to many species, breeds, and temperaments. Furthermore, my experiences over the years, have led me to progress to working as a Veterinarian Assistant in daytime practices, as well as a emergency clinic. Throughout all my years in animal care, I’ve taken a particular interest in animal behavior. This interest has led me to pursue a personal goal in becoming a dog trainer with Good Dog, and utilizing every chance to interact with dogs, and animals in general. Whether it is pet sitting, dog walking, or training, I am here to help!
